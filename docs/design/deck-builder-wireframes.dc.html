cached · rev 42

The plan

Tessa Fatestitcher untaps your rocks and reanimates suspend spells, pushing an artifact-heavy midrange deck toward explosive turns where a single ramp piece snowballs into free counters and card advantage.

Synergies

Sol Ring and Arcane Signet feed Tessa's untap trigger; Rhystic Study and Phyrexian Arena keep the grip full so removal is always available when the table turns on you.

Interaction

Swords to Plowshares, Go for the Throat and Beast Within cover creatures across colors; hold up counter mana once Tessa is online.

Weaknesses

Light on board wipes (3) and vulnerable to artifact removal — losing your mana rocks strands the reanimation plan a turn behind.
